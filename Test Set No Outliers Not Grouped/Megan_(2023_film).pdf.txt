Megan (2023 film)
Megan (stylized as M3GAN or MΞGAN) is an upcoming
 science-fiction [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Horror_film"] horror film directed by Gerard Johnstone from a
screenplay by [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akela_Cooper"] Akela Cooper and a story by [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Wan"] James Wan. It stars
 Allison Williams and [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ronny_Chieng"] Ronny Chieng.[1][2][3]
Megan is scheduled to be theatrically released in the United
States on January 13, 2023, by [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Universal_Pictures"] Universal Pictures.

Premise
The story follows Gemma ([URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Allison_Williams_(actress)"] Allison Williams),  a brilliant
roboticist at a toy company who uses artificial intelligence to
develop M3GAN, a life-like doll programmed to be a kid's
greatest companion and a  parent's  greatest ally. After
unexpectedly gaining custody of her orphaned niece, Gemma
enlists the help of the M3GAN prototype a decision that has
unimaginable consequences.
