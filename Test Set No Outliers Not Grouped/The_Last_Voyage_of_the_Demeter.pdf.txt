The Last Voyage of the Demeter
 The Last Voyage of the Demeter is an upcoming American horror
film directed by [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andr%C3%A9_%C3%98vredal"] André Øvredal from a screenplay by Bragi F.
Schut, Stefan Ruzowitsky, and Zak Olkewicz. It is an adaptation
of "The Captain's Log", a chapter from the 1897 [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bram_Stoker"] Bram Stoker
 novel Dracula.[4] The film stars Corey Hawkins, Aisling
Franciosi, [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liam_Cunningham"] Liam Cunningham, and [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Dastmalchian"] David Dastmalchian.
The Last Voyage of the Demeter is set to be released in the United
States on August 11, 2023, by [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Universal_Pictures"] Universal Pictures.[5]

 In 2002, Bragi F. Schut wrote the initial screenplay for The Last Voyage of the Demeter with Robert
Schwentke on board to direct. However, the film languished in [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Development_hell"] development hell for close to two decades
with several other directors attached to the film including [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marcus_Nispel"] Marcus Nispel, Stefan Ruzowitsky, [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Slade"] David Slade
 and Neil Marshall. At one point Noomi Rapace and Ben Kingsley were set to star in the film, with Viggo
Mortensen "in talks".[6][7][8][9]
In October 2019, it was announced [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andr%C3%A9_%C3%98vredal"] André Øvredal would direct the film with [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amblin_Partners"] Amblin Partners set to
produce.[10] In January 2021, [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corey_Hawkins"] Corey Hawkins joined the cast of the film with a screenplay by Zak
 Olkewicz.[11] In June 2021, David Dastmalchian, Liam Cunningham, Aisling Franciosi, Javier Botet, Jon
Jon Briones, [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stefan_Kapi%C4%8Di%C4%87"] Stefan Kapičić, [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nikolai_Nikolaeff"] Nikolai Nikolaeff, [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Woody_Norman"] Woody Norman, Martin Furulund and [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chris_Walley_(actor)"] Chris Walley joined
the cast of the film.[12]
 Principal photography began in June 2021 in Berlin. Filming later occurred in Malta.[13]Principal
photography has wrapped, as of 1 October 2021.[14]
