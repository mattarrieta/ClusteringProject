The Boogeyman (2023 film)
The Boogeyman is an upcoming American [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Horror_film"] horror film directed by
 Rob Savage and written by Scott Beck, Bryan Woods, Akela
 Cooper, and Mark Heyman, based on the 1978 short story of the
 same name by Stephen King. It stars Chris Messina, Sophie
Thatcher, [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Dastmalchian"] David Dastmalchian, and [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marin_Ireland"] Marin Ireland. The film is
scheduled to be released on [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hulu"] Hulu in 2023.[1]

Production
The Boogeyman is a horror adaptation of [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stephen_King"] Stephen King's 1978 [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Boogeyman_(short_story)"] short story of the same name. On June 26,
2018, it was announced filmmaking partners [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scott_Beck"] Scott Beck and [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bryan_Woods"] Bryan Woods would write the screenplay with
 Shawn Levy, [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dan_Levine"] Dan Levine, and Dan Cohen attached to produce for [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/21_Laps_Entertainment"] 21 Laps Entertainment.[3] However, in
2019, after the [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acquisition_of_21st_Century_Fox_by_Disney"] Disney acquisition of 20th Century Fox, the film was canceled, along with other films in
development.[4] In November 2021, the film was revived, and it was reported that Rob Savage would
[URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chris_Messina"]
Page 2
 direct from a screenplay written by Mark Heyman, based on original drafts by Beck, Woods, and Akela
 Cooper.[2] In January 2022, Sophie Thatcher and Chris Messina joined the cast.[5] In February, David
Dastmalchian, [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marin_Ireland"] Marin Ireland, Vivien Lyra Blair, and [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Madison_Hu"] Madison Hu were added to the cast.[6] Filming began
in February 2022 in [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Orleans"] New Orleans.[6]
Release
The Boogeyman is scheduled to be released on Hulu in 2023.[6]
