Basic Purpose: 
Provides needed labor to maintain park facilities. Operates park maintenance equipment to perform
necessary maintenance to athletic fields, active and passive recreation areas, and park related areas. 
Major Responsibilities: 
Must be able to perform normal labor associated with park maintenance, including, but not limited to, 
painting, mowing, trimming, trash removal, ball field maintenance, raking, cleaning and general
landscaping type activities. 
Familiar with the operation of power equipment, including blowers, trimmers, mowers, leaf vacuums, 
paint machines, infield groomers, rollers, generators. Responsible for the proper care and
maintenance of equipment assigned. 
Familiar with safe operations of all equipment. 
May be asked to work independently under certain conditions with little supervision. Must be able to
work as a lead worker for certain assignments. 
Must be able to work shifts as required and work under varying weather conditions. Must be available
during all seasons as needed. 
Job Qualifications: 
Applicant must be at least eighteen (18) years of age, and have experience sufficient to indicate the
aptitude to do the work safely and efficiently. 
Must be licensed to operate a motor vehicle as provided under Chapter 90, Section 8, of the
Massachusetts General Laws with Class D license as designated by the Registrar.
