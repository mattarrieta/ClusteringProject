Lifeguards - Beaches
Parks & Recreation
$18.44 - $22.67 hourly
Monday - Sunday 10:00 AM – 7:00 PM
Accountability: 
Supervising the general conduct of beach patrons, preventing accidents, and reacting to emergency
situations 
General Responsibilities: 
• Perform lifesaving and first aid duties and ensure safety of beach patrons at all times 
• Responsible for daily maintenance of bathhouse, bathroom and changing rooms, and grounds 
• Enforce all beach rules and regulations 
• Spot hazards or potential hazards and take measures to prevent accidents 
• Participate in pre-season training, staff training sessions and conditioning program 
• Participate in emergency drills and trainings 
• Is courteous, consistent, helpful and informative to beach patrons 
• Must be extremely alert and exhibit a high degree of awareness 
• Works as a team player with other staff members 
• Perform any tasks necessary for beach operations as described by the Beach Director or Head 
Guard 
• Insure the proper use and night time storage of all safety equipment, beach supplies and equipment 
Knowledge, Skill & Ability: 
Knowledge of rules and regulations pertaining to waterfront safety; understanding of standard
lifeguard practices; ability to perform skills as needed to prevent or respond to emergencies; ability to
maintain discipline and command respect from the general public; ability to function as part of a team
with the beach staff 
Experience and Training: 
Minimum age 16. Current certifications in American Red Cross (or comparable association) in the
following: Lifeguard Training; CPR and First Aid for the Professional Rescuer. Prior lifeguarding
experience is preferred.
