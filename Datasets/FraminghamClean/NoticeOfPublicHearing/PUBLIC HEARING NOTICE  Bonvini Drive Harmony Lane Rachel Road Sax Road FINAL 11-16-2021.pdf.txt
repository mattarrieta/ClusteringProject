NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARING
CITY OF FRAMINGHAM
STREET ACCEPTANCES
The City Council of the City of Framingham, Massachusetts will hold a public hearing
on Tuesday, November 16, 2021, at 7:00 p.m. in the Blumer Meeting Room at the
Memorial Building, 150 Concord Street, Framingham, MA 01702, in accordance with
the provisions of the General Laws of Massachusetts Chapters 79 and 82, and Chapter
410 of the Acts of 2014 to consider the laying out and acceptance of the following as
public ways: Bonvini Drive, Harmony Lane, Rachel Road, and Sax Road as shown
on the plans on file with the Department of Public Works, to determine whether it is in
the public interest to accept these road as public ways and to hear all persons interested
in the acceptance of said ways.  Members of the public may also participate in the City
Council’s public hearing on November 16, 2021, 07:00 PM Eastern Time (US and
Canada) via the Zoom teleconferencing application.
