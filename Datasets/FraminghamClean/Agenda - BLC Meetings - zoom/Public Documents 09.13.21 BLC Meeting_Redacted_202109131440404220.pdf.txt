Meeting Minutes  Board of License Commissioners 
Mr. Pearlman introduced himself as the legal representative of Nancy Liquors Inc seeking a liquor
transfer from CBIII Inc. Mr. Pearlman explained that Mr. Sureshbhai Patel is the owner of Nancy Liquors
which has been partially financed by Rockland Trust, he has experience in the retail business and have
other locations across the state. The Manager of record is Mr. Gopi Patel and the Assistant Manager is
Suresh Patel both are Alcohol Served Trained. 
Ms. Medrano made a motion to approve the Transfer All Alcohol Retail Store License and Pledge of
Liquor License and Inventory from CBIII Enterprises LLC to Nancy Liquors with new proposed hours of
Monday –Saturday9a.m. to 11 p.m.; Sunday 10a.m. to10p.m. 
Ms. Wadland seconded the motion. 
All voted in favor, 4-0. 
Wadland: Aye
Porter: Aye
Pologe: Aye
Medrano: Aye
