NEW BUSINESS
Report of Police Department procedures for potential alcohol violations. 
Lt. McCarthy is charges of the Licensing operations for the police department and made a presentation on what is
going behind the scenes when addressing potential violations. 
Shift commanders notify him of events that involve establishment or alcohol consumption. He reviews reports
and follow up and does investigations, he talks to the establishment management to prevent it from becoming a
violation issue and addressed in front of the board. MS. Wadland commented about the possibility to bring in
front of the board the manager of an establishment if they are frequently in the local news, but at the same time
the board do not want to interfere with the police investigation. Lt McCarthy stated that his first course of action
is to contact the manager of record to discuss the issues. He had talked to several of establishments. Ms. Potter
stated that she is opposed to restrict hours if there are no documented violations.
