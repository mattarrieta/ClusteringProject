Shelby Oaks
Shelby Oaks is an upcoming American [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supernatural_horror_film"] supernatural horror
 mystery film produced, written, and directed by [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chris_Stuckmann"] Chris Stuckmann
 in his feature directorial debut. It stars Camille Sullivan, Brendan
Sexton III, [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Beach"] Michael Beach, [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robin_Bartlett"] Robin Bartlett, [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keith_David"] Keith David, Charlie
Talbert, Emily Bennett, and Sarah Durn. The film is inspired by an
online marketing campaign about a  fictional paranormal
investigative team called The Paranormal Paranoids. Produced by
Paper Street Pictures, the film is set to be released on July 2023.

Development
In July 2021, [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/YouTuber"] YouTuber and film critic [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chris_Stuckmann"] Chris Stuckmann signed on
to write and direct a [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supernatural_horror_film"] supernatural horror [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mystery_film"] mystery film titled Shelby
Oaks for Paper Street Pictures.[5] The film is based on an online
marketing campaign depicting series of found footage videos about
a fictional paranormal investigative team named The Paranormal
 Paranoids.[6] The film will be produced by Stuckmann, Aaron B.
Koontz, Cameron Burns, and Ashleigh Snead.[7]
Funding for the film was achieved via a [URL: "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kickstarter"] Kickstarter campaign that
began on March 1, 2022. On March 21, Shelby Oaks became the
most-funded horror film project on Kickstarter after raising
$650,000.[8] The campaign crossed the $1 million mark from
11,200 backers by March 25.[9] The cast was revealed at the
 Cannes Film Festival in May 2022.[4]
